How to Make a Brand Name That Works (With AI Examples)

Choosing the right brand name is one of the most important steps when starting a new business, product, or service. A good name is memorable, meaningful, and aligned with your brand's identity.

💡 What Makes a Great Name?

  • ✨ Memorability: Easy to remember and say.
  • 🎯 Relevance: Connected to your brand’s mission or values.
  • 🔍 Searchability: Easy to find online, with available domain/social handles.
  • 🧠 Emotional Impact: Evokes feeling or imagery.

🤖 How AI Can Help with Naming

AI naming tools can generate creative name ideas based on your keywords, industry, and desired brand image. They help speed up the brainstorming process and spark inspiration.

📌 Tips for Using AI Naming Tools

  • 🧩 Try different prompts (keywords, traits, emotions).
  • ⛳ Use filters (gender-neutral, modern, traditional).
  • 📈 Check domain and social handle availability.
  • 🗣 Say it out loud — does it sound good?

📝 Final Thoughts

There’s no perfect name — only the one that fits your story best. With the right mix of creativity and AI, you can find a brand name that works.
