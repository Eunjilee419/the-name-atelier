什么是四柱命理？

四柱命理是基于出生年份、月份、日期和时辰（共四柱）来推测一个人命运的韩国传统命理体系。

什么是四柱命理？

Saju (사주) is the traditional Korean fortune-telling system also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny. It analyzes a person's destiny based on the year, month, day, and hour of birth using the Chinese zodiac and the theory of Yin, Yang, and the Five Elements (Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, Water).

🔮 The Four Pillars

  • Year Pillar (年柱): Influences from ancestors and early life
  • Month Pillar (月柱): Family and career tendencies
  • Day Pillar (日柱): Core personality
  • Hour Pillar (時柱): Future and personal ambitions
